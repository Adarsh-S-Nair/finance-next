At {{brandName}}, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our financial management platform.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you use {{brandName}}, we collect:

- **Account information.** Name, email address, and profile avatar (when you sign in via Google OAuth).
- **Financial account data.** Connected via Plaid — account names, balances, account types, and institution details.
- **Transaction data.** Transaction descriptions, amounts, dates, categories, and merchant names from your linked accounts.
- **Investment holdings.** Where you've connected brokerage accounts, your holdings and investment transaction history.
- **Payment information.** If you subscribe to Pro, billing information is collected and processed by Stripe; we store only your Stripe customer ID and subscription status.
- **Usage data.** How you interact with the service, pages visited, features used, and errors encountered.
- **Device information.** Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the service
- Display your financial information and analytics
- Process subscription payments
- Send service-related notifications and product updates
- Improve and personalize your experience
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information as defined under applicable law. We share data only with service providers strictly necessary to operate the platform, under contractual obligations to protect user data, and only for the purposes described below. We may also disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.

## 4. Plaid Data Collection & Consent

When you connect a financial account, we use Plaid Inc. to facilitate the connection. By using the service, you grant {{brandName}} and Plaid the right to collect your account information from your financial institution and share it with {{brandName}} for the purposes described in this policy. Plaid's collection and use of your data is governed by its own privacy policy.

- [Plaid's End User Privacy Policy](https://plaid.com/legal/#end-user-privacy-policy)
- [Plaid Portal](https://my.plaid.com) — review connections and revoke access at any time

We do not store your banking credentials. Plaid stores them encrypted on its own infrastructure; {{brandName}} only receives tokens that allow us to request account data on your behalf.

## 5. Subprocessors

We use the following third-party services to operate the platform. Each has its own privacy policy governing its handling of your data:

- **Supabase** — database and authentication
- **Vercel** — application hosting
- **Plaid** — financial account connections
- **Stripe** — payment processing
- **Google** — OAuth sign-in (when you choose that option)
- **Sentry** — error monitoring (IP address, browser, URL, stack traces)
- **Axiom** — server log aggregation
- **Finnhub, CoinGecko, Yahoo Finance** — market data (no personal data shared)

## 6. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember your preferences, and measure how the service is used. Essential cookies (authentication, session state) are required for the service to function. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from signing in or using the service.

## 7. Data Security

We implement the following security measures:

- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2 or higher
- Data at rest is encrypted via our hosting provider's standard encryption (AES-256)
- Banking credentials are never stored on our systems; Plaid handles them
- Access to production systems is restricted and audit-logged
- We use automated error and anomaly monitoring to detect suspicious activity

No security system is perfect. While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

- **Account data.** For as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your personal data from active systems within 30 days.
- **Transaction history.** For the life of your account, to provide historical analytics. Deleted when you close your account.
- **Backups.** Deleted personal data may persist in encrypted backups for up to 90 days before being permanently removed.
- **Legal and financial records.** We may retain certain data longer where required by law (e.g., payment and tax records) — typically up to 7 years.
- **Server logs.** Operational logs are retained for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Our hosting providers (Supabase, Vercel) may store and process data in the United States and other countries. If you access the service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to, processed in, and stored in the US. Where data is transferred from the EU or UK, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission.

## 10. Legal Basis for Processing

Where the GDPR applies, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

- **Contract** — processing necessary to provide the service you requested
- **Legitimate interest** — improving the service, preventing fraud, and security monitoring
- **Consent** — where you have given explicit consent (e.g., optional communications)
- **Legal obligation** — complying with tax, accounting, or other applicable laws

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the CPRA, gives you the following rights:

- **Right to know** what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and share
- **Right to delete** personal information we have collected about you
- **Right to correct** inaccurate personal information
- **Right to opt out** of the sale or sharing of your personal information (see §14)
- **Right to limit use** of sensitive personal information
- **Right to non-discrimination** — we will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

We have not sold personal information in the preceding 12 months and do not currently sell personal information as defined under the CCPA. If this changes, we will update this policy and provide the required opt-out mechanism.
